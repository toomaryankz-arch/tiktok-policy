Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last Updated: March 2026

This document governs the use of the TikTok Content Posting API integrated through the Make.com automation platform.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from your TikTok account. The application only accesses the necessary information to upload and publish video content (video files, titles, and descriptions) as configured by the user in their Make.com scenarios.

2. Use of Data

All data accessed via the TikTok API is used exclusively for the purpose of automated content publishing. We do not use your content for any other purposes, including training AI models or third-party marketing.

3. Data Retention and Security

We do not store your TikTok login credentials. Authentication is handled securely through the official TikTok OAuth 2.0 protocol. Access tokens are managed by Make.com and are never visible to the developer of this integration.

4. User Rights

You can revoke the application's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok account security settings or by deleting the connection in your Make.com dashboard.

5. Compliance

This integration complies with the TikTok Developer Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact us via this GitHub repository's contact options.
